Food

Candlelight Inn — Chicken George ↗

Home of the original Chicken George: hand-battered, deep-fried chicken tenders served with Jan's sauce — a tangy vinegar-and-mayo house special that's been top secret since the 1970s.

📍 Rock Falls location
Heads up: the original Sterling Candlelight Inn closed at the end of 2023. The Rock Falls location, just across the river, is the nearest one still serving Chicken George.
DinnerAngelo's II
Food

Angelo's II — Spaghetti Pizza ↗

The Sterling institution, open since 1982. The spaghetti pizza is the whole reason for the stop — a deep-crust pie topped with spaghetti, sausage, and mozzarella. Rich, legendary, and one slice genuinely does the job.

📍 2525 E Lincolnway, Sterling, IL🕐 11 AM–9 PM daily
Local tip: the salad bar is part of the tradition — order the Angelo's Special and grab garlic bread on the side.
